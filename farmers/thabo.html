Pako Ramatla runs a family-owned dairy farm in the rolling grasslands of Mafeteng. With over 30 Holstein and Jersey cows, his focus is on producing high-quality, fresh milk and handcrafted cheese.


His father started the farm over 25 years ago, and Pako took over operations after agricultural studies at the National University of Lesotho. He implemented better feeding practices, introduced rotational grazing to keep pastures healthy, and began producing artisanal cheese for local restaurants.


Pako’s dairy products have won awards at local agricultural shows, and he’s committed to keeping prices fair so that even low-income households can enjoy fresh milk. His farm also serves as a training center for agricultural students, offering hands-on experience in animal husbandry.
