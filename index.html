Our Farmers

Browse Lesotho’s finest farmers.

From small beginnings to bountiful harvests, meet Thabo Mokhethi — a passionate crop farmer specializing in potatoes and beans. His hard work and innovation keep his fields flourishing year-round

Meet ‘M’e Mpho Tselanyane, a dedicated crop farmer from the fertile fields of Leribe. Known for her vibrant maize and fresh vegetables, she’s built a loyal community of customers who value her quality produce and warm service.

For rich, creamy dairy products and the friendliest herd in Lesotho, look no further than Pako Ramatla’s dairy farm in Mafeteng. His milk and cheese are a local favorite.

Raising some of the healthiest sheep in the highlands, Teboho Ntlama produces premium wool and mutton prized both locally and internationally.
